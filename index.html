AboutFruit Loop

What is Fruit Loop PVD?

In the most literal sense, the Fruit Loop is a series of LGBTQ+ bars and nightclubs in downtown Providence, all within easy walking distance of each other — at least in the summer when it's nice out. The name has been around since at least 2006, though I'll be honest, I'm not entirely sure where it came from.

In a bigger sense, the Fruit Loop is the thriving LGBTQIA+ community of Providence itself. A celebration of queer joy in the smallest big city in the country.

And in the sense of this site and our social media pages, it's a resource to help us find each other, support each other, and celebrate each other — to lift up our voices together and magnify our impact and our visibility.

Hi, I'm Graham.

I'll be honest with you — I created this out of a little bit of pettiness. My friends could never remember which events we were going to on a given weekend, and some weekends there is a lot going on. But from that pettiness, Fruit Loop PVD has grown into something really spectacular — a reflection of how truly incredible the LGBTQIA+ community of this city is.
